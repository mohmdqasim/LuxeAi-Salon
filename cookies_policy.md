## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function properly, work more efficiently, and provide reporting information.

- **Session Cookies:**  
  Temporary cookies that expire and are automatically deleted when you close your browser.

- **Persistent Cookies:**  
  Cookies that remain on your device until they expire or are manually deleted. These help us understand usage patterns and improve the website experience.

## 3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

- **Authentication & Security:** To log you into GlamMate AI and keep you logged in while navigating the site.  
- **Payments:** To process secure transactions via Stripe.  
- **Preferences:** To remember your settings (e.g., selected language or theme).  
- **Analytics:** To understand how users interact with our AI features so we can improve the product.

These cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are typically set in response to actions taken by you, such as logging in or filling out forms.

| Cookie Name        | Provider      | Purpose                                                  |

## 5. Controlling Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to accept or reject cookies.

- **Cookie Banner:**  
  On your first visit, you can choose to “Accept All” or “Reject Non-Essential” cookies. You may update your preferences at any time via the website footer.

- **Browser Controls:**  
  You can configure your browser to accept or refuse cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may limit certain functionality, such as staying logged in.
